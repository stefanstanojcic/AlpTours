Our Mission

Explore the mountain universe in several different ways.

Our goal is to make you live the extreme-sport experience of a lifetime! Our tours are unique not just because the trails are awesome and the sceneries breathtaking but because we put all our heart, passion and know-how in the preparation of our trips. We specialize in guiding small groups of between 2 to 8 clients per guide, no more! Why? Firstly, for safety and security, secondly for cohesion within the group relating to technical and fitness levels and thirdly to make the tours more friendly. We put your safety first. And we are also Mountainneers, we all practice mountain biking of course but also trekking, skiing, ski touring, high mountaineering, climbing, trail running, snowshoeing. Our passion for the Mountain makes us nature lovers and we find it a real pleasure to share our passion for flora, fauna, geology, local history and architecture with you. Excellent way to discover everything the mountains and countryside have to offer with open eyes, we will energetically point out as much as possible!

Hiking

Hiking is a form of walking with the specific purpose of exploring and enjoying natural beauties. Walking through natural areas that involves carrying the necessary equipment and all that is needed is a very popular recreational activity.

Mountaineering

Mountaineering is the set of activities that involves ascending mountains. It has branched into specializations that address different aspects of mountains,depending on whether the route chosen is over rock, snow, or ice or on level ground.

Mountain biking

Mountain biking requires endurance, core strength and balance, bike handling skills, and self-reliance. In the case of freeride, downhill, and dirt jumping, aerial maneuvers are performed off both natural features and specially constructed jumps and ramps.

Best selling packages

The Inca Trail

The most famous trek of all has to be classic four day Inca Trail. Originally built to connect the city of Cusco with the lost city of Machu Picchu. You begin at kilometer 82 and trek to the small village of Huallabamba to spend your first night under the stars of the Andes.

Ride the Alps
